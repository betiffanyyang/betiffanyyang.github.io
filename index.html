Welcome to SpeakSquid

Are you ready to practice the most important part of learning a language--speaking!!

SpeakSquid uses AI to generate conversations from the real world that you can practice from the comfort of your home!!

Functionalities

Speaking Practice

Currently available in 57 languages, practice speaking with AI to enhance communication skills in the langauge you are learning

Track Progress

SpeakSquid has a video feature that enables you to record and track your progress overtime and motivate you to continue

AI Conversations

Powered by AI, SpeakSquid is able to respond with custom prompts to allow personalization of practice

Corrections and Translations

Transcriptions are provided to enhance the learning experience, including: correcting grammar, translation, and native pronounciation
